## Key findings

- Only **~3% of 80M creators** monetise meaningfully,the monetisation gap is the biggest product opportunity
- **Vernacular creators** (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu) are growing 3–5x faster than English-language
- **India's UPI stack** enables micro-transactions that don't work in Western markets creating India-first monetisation models with no global equivalent
- The **micro-creator segment** (10K–100K followers, ₹50K–₹3L/month) is the most underserved by existing platforms
- **Paid communities** have the highest LTV of any monetisation model at the mid-tier creator level

## Platform comparison methodology

SuperProfile, Gumroad, Beacons, and Stan Store scored across:
- India-market fit (UPI support, INR pricing, vernacular support)
- Creator UX
- Monetisation depth
- Distribution leverage

## Data sources

Public reports from RedSeer, BCG, FICCI, EY, company filings, and industry interviews (2024–2025). All figures are estimates.

## Why I built this

I ran creator partnership campaigns at Cosmofeed/SuperProfile for 9 months and kept seeing a gap between how platforms positioned themselves and how creators actually used them. This dashboard is my attempt to map the landscape structurally useful for founders, investors, and creators trying to understand where the market is going.
